    About Us

    Savio Global Ventures

    Savio Global Ventures is a division of Starlight Global Ventures, a leading supplier to the construction industry.

    We are a dynamic and fast-growing company specializing in the distribution of treated wood, engineered boards, and all kinds of electrical equipment for building construction. Our mission is to provide top-quality materials while actively responding to customer feedback.

    With over five years of hands-on industry experience, Savio Global Ventures has earned the trust of more than 1,000 satisfied customers through a commitment to quality, reliability, and service excellence. Our success has led to the establishment of three branches nationwide, with continued plans for growth.
